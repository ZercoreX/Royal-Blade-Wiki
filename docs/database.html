Items

In Royal-Blade, Each universe of the game has its own unique set of item drops. The Level & Rarity of the items greatly affect their attributes, critical stats, and overall usefulness.


Weapons:

    Mobs

    Each universe of the game has its own unique set of mobs.

    Mobs are categorized into several types, each offering different challenges and rewards.

    Mob Types

    • Normal Mobs

      Standard enemies that are commonly encountered on each floor.

    • Mini-Bosses

      Stronger enemies that drop valuable items and must be defeated to obtain the Boss Key, which is required to access the boss room. Defeating mini-bosses is also a requirement for unlocking the next universe.

    • Area Bosses (Coming Soon)

      Powerful enemies that act as gatekeepers to the next universe. Defeating the boss is a mandatory step to advance further in the game.

    • Floor Boss

      Special bosses that guard specific zones and provide unique rewards when defeated.

    Boss Room Mechanics

    Once you unlock the boss room using the Boss Key and enter, you will not be able to leave until the boss is killed. After defeating the boss and leaving the room, you cannot re-enter until the boss respawns. However, if you remain in the room after killing the boss, you will be kicked out once the boss begins its respawn process, ensuring a fair reset for the encounter.
