Overview

Welcome to the official Royal-Blade wiki, your comprehensive guide to everything in the world of Royal-Blade!


Royal-Blade is an immersive open-world RPG developed by Royal Verse Studio and founded by </ Zercore >. Set in a vast, intricately designed world, players will embark on epic quests, discover hidden secrets, and battle formidable enemies as they progress through a rich storyline filled with diverse characters and challenging environments.


Currently in its Early Access phase, Royal-Blade is actively being developed, with regular updates and new features being added. This wiki serves as the only official source for gameplay information, mechanics, and in-depth guides, created and maintained by Zercore, the game's developer. Whether you're new to the game or a seasoned adventurer, this wiki aims to simplify and enhance your gameplay experience.


This wiki is not only a tool for players but a living, evolving resource that will grow alongside the game as new updates, expansions, and features are introduced. Stay tuned for the latest information and insider tips as Royal-Blade’s development progresses. Whether you’re seeking lore, tips for boss fights, or insight into new content, the Royal-Blade wiki has everything you need.
